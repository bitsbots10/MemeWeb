Frequently Asked Questions

How does the First Ledger Extension detect new listings?

Our extension continuously monitors the XRPL DEXs through direct node connections, allowing us to detect new token listings within milliseconds of their appearance on the ledger.

Is my wallet information secure?

Absolutely. Your private keys never leave your browser. All trading operations are conducted locally, and we never store or transmit your sensitive wallet information.

How do I set up automated trading?

In the extension settings, you can define criteria like maximum investment amount, minimum liquidity, and token characteristics. When new tokens meeting these criteria are detected, the extension will automatically execute trades according to your parameters.

Can I use the extension on mobile?

Currently, the extension is available for desktop browsers only. We're developing a mobile app version that will be available soon.

How do I upgrade to the Premium version?

You can upgrade directly within the extension by clicking on the "Upgrade" button and completing the payment with MEME tokens. The upgrade is instant, and you'll immediately gain access to all premium features.
